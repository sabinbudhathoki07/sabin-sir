VIP lounge

If you hold a First Class or Business Class ticket, or you are a Sky Pearl Gold Card or Silver Card member, you may use the Budgets Airlines VIP lounge at airports that have signed a VIP lounge usage agreement with China Southern Airlines.


Seat Reservation

Can you reserve my seat for longer if I have checked in online?

No, you must arrive at the boarding gate 15 minutes before departure; otherwise, it will be regarded as a voluntary waiver of the seat and your ticket will be dealt with as a voluntary change or refund.


Boarding Pass

Is there a time limit for getting my boarding pass?

Different flight routes and airports have different check-in deadlines. Generally, the check-in closing time is 30 minutes before departure, but in some airports it is 45 minutes or one hour before departure. We therefore suggest you check the relevant information before traveling, so as to leave ample time for check-in.Please consult the local airport for any questions.


Coupon

What is a coupon?

A coupon is a promotional code issued by budgetflights website and consisting of 12 numbers and letters with a certain value and validity period. Each passenger can use only one coupon per flight segment. In order to obtain promotional codes, please look out for promotions on the budget flights website.
