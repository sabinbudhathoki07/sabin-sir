What you'll need

Be ready to enter either your booking ID and personal contact email OR your booking ID and passenger last name. Click here to check-in online now.

After you've checked in

Once you've successfully checked-in, we recommend you to:

Print your boarding pass(es)

Make sure you have all necessary travel documents

Make a note in your diary to arrive at the airport two hours early

Remember:

if you modify your booking, you'll need to check-in online again, as your original boarding pass may no longer be valid.

Check-in at the airport with our friendly team

If you prefer to check-in personally at the airport, no problem. We ask you come to see us at the check-in desk two hours before your scheduled departure time. This gives us enough time to arrange take-off and means you won't have to rush!

What we'll check

Be ready for us to check: your ticket your valid ID or passport the weight of any check-in baggage you'd like to put in the hold the weight and size of any carry-on baggage you'd like with you in the cabin

After you've checked in

Once you've successfully checked-in, we recommend you listen to all announcements and keep an eye on the departures screens. If you need any information or assistance, just ask a member of the Blue Air team.
